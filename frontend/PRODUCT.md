# Product

## Register

product

## Users

- **老人 (Elderly)**: 60+ years old, varying tech literacy. Need large text, simple flows, emergency access. Primary job: monitor health, book services, call for help.
- **家属 (Family)**: Adult children caring for aging parents remotely. Check health data, pay for services, receive alerts. Primary job: stay informed, pay bills, respond to emergencies.
- **管理员 (Admin)**: Community staff managing users, devices, services, orders, emergencies. Primary job: oversee operations, respond to incidents.

## Product Purpose

智慧社区养老平台 (Smart Community Elderly Care) connects elderly residents, their families, and community staff in one platform. Health monitoring, service booking, emergency response, and family oversight — reducing the care gap between family members and their aging parents.

## Brand Personality

**温暖可信 · 亲和关怀 · 安全可靠** (Warm & Trustworthy · Caring & Approachable · Safe & Reliable)

## Anti-references

- Clinical hospital UIs (cold, sterile, intimidating)
- Complex data dashboards (overwhelming for elderly)
- Generic SaaS templates (stripes, gradients, emoji-heavy)
- Dark mode (reduces readability for elderly eyes)

## Design Principles

1. **Clarity over cleverness** — Large text, high contrast, obvious CTAs
2. **One tap to safety** — Emergency access always one action away
3. **Family peace of mind** — At-a-glance health status, proactive alerts
4. **Warm but not saccharine** — Professional trust with human warmth
5. **Accessible by default** — WCAG AA minimum, touch targets ≥48px

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA compliance
- Minimum touch target 48px
- Large font sizes (body ≥16px)
- High contrast text (≥4.5:1 body, ≥3:1 large)
- Reduced motion support
- Screen reader compatible
